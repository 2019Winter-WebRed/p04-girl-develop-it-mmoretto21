About

Girl Develop It is a nonprofit organization that exists to provide affordable and judgement-free opportunities for women interested in learning web and software development. Through in-person classes and community support, Girl Develop It helps women of diverse backgrounds achieve their technology goals and build confidance in their careers and their everyday lives.
